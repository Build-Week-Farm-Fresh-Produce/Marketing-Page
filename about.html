About Us

Welcome to our Showcase Project! Our love for non-GMO produce brought us together. Our passion for coding created this project. Our team consists of five software engineers who developed Farm Fresh Produce to demonstate the collaboration of different levels of expertise. Today, we continue to grow our knowledge at Lambda, encouraging strong skills for a better Internet for all.
