Fresh From the Farm, Organic Produce

We believe everyone should have access to organic produce. We partner with local farms and give opportunity for the surrounding communities to have access to them in an easy and convenient way.

Pick Your Farmer

We show you what farms are available in your area. Have a farm you would love to support? We give you the opportunity to shop directly from your favorite farms selection of produce.

Choose Your Produce

Fill your shopping cart with all your favorite organic produce! We make it easy for farmers to update their inventory so you always have access to the most fresh and delicious organic produce!

Pick Up Your Order

When your order is ready we will let you know what beautiful farm you can visit to pick it up! We take pride in this part of the process and love when farms can interact directly with their customers.
